# NudgeHQ Simple One-Page Summary

## What NudgeHQ is

NudgeHQ is a work app for teams.

It helps companies know:
- who is working on what
- who is stuck
- what got done today
- where a manager needs to step in

In simple words:
NudgeHQ helps teams stay updated without chasing people again and again on WhatsApp, calls, or random chats.

## Our motto

Less chasing. More clarity. Better work.

## Our aim

Our aim is to make daily work simple, clear, and easy to manage for everyone in a company.

We want employees, managers, and founders to all stay on the same page without confusion.

## Our vision

Our vision is to build a smarter work system where:
- updates are easy
- managers get clarity fast
- teams feel less pressure
- work moves with better focus and less chaos

We want work communication to feel organized, calm, and useful, not stressful or messy.

## The main problem we solve

In many teams, managers waste time asking:
- "Any update?"
- "Who is blocked?"
- "Who finished the task?"
- "Why did nobody tell me this earlier?"

Employees also get confused because work updates are scattered in many places.

NudgeHQ puts all of this in one clean space.

## What employees do in NudgeHQ

Employees can:
- give daily updates
- share wins
- mention blockers
- manage their own tasks
- use their personal space to stay organized

Inside NudgeSpace, employees can use:
- **Social**: a team sharing space, like a mix of updates, quick posts, and team interaction
- **U Space**: a personal area to manage focus, tasks, and day planning

So one part is for team energy, and one part is for personal productivity.

## What managers do in NudgeHQ

Managers get a clear dashboard that shows:
- who updated today
- which tasks need attention
- who is blocked
- which work needs proof before review
- what needs action right now

This means a manager does not have to manually follow up with every person.

## What admins or founders do in NudgeHQ

Admins/founders can:
- set up teams
- invite people
- manage departments
- see overall team activity
- make sure work is moving properly

It gives them better visibility without checking 10 different tools.

## What NudgeAI does

NudgeAI is the smart helper inside NudgeHQ.

It helps by:
- turning messy thoughts into clean updates
- helping write summaries
- helping managers understand what matters fast
- making updates more clear and useful

Simple meaning:
it saves time and reduces confusion.

## New things we have added

We have improved the manager experience with features like:
- **Manager Morning Brief**: quick summary of what needs attention today
- **No Update Alert**: shows who has not shared their update
- **One-click Escalate Blocker**: helps raise important issues fast
- **Proof Required Tasks**: shows which tasks need links, files, or screenshots
- **Admin Setup Checklist**: helps new companies set up properly
- **Update Quality Score**: encourages better updates
- **Smart Task Templates**: makes repeated task creation faster
- better graphs and clearer dashboard visuals

## What makes NudgeHQ different

NudgeHQ is not just a task tool.

It mixes:
- team updates
- task tracking
- manager visibility
- AI help
- personal planning

So it feels more like a daily work operating system, not just another boring dashboard.

work should not feel so messy.

Instead of making teams report in complicated ways, NudgeHQ makes updates feel lighter, faster, and more natural.
